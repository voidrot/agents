# Interaction Design Full Guidance

This reference preserves the long-form guidance layer required by the update plan while the active `SKILL.md` remains a concise routing entrypoint. Use the more focused companion references for deeper examples and detailed procedures.

## Detailed guidance

- Tie every interaction to a user need: feedback, affordance, progress, continuity, confirmation, or recovery.
- Specify states explicitly: idle, hover, focus, pressed, loading, success, error, empty, disabled, interrupted, and restored.
- Choose timing, easing, and motion distance to clarify cause and effect without delaying completion or causing vestibular discomfort.
- Validate reduced motion, keyboard paths, focus visibility, screen-reader announcements, performance, cancellation, and edge states.
